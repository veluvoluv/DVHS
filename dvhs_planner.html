🌐 UC Scout
UC-approved A-G courses. Self-paced online. ~$200/course. Best for humanities and social science APs. Check specific course approval with counselor.
📘 BYU Independent Study
Nationally accredited. Self-paced. Accepted by most CSUs — verify with each UC campus for transferability. Some courses need counselor pre-approval.
🏛️ DVC / LMC (Diablo Valley / Los Medanos)
Community college. Free with dual enrollment. Courses earn college credit + may satisfy A-G. DVHS counselor must pre-approve before enrollment.
